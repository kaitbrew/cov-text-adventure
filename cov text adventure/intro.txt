You're awake again. It's the same apartment again. You're told it smells like cigarette smoke, but you don't notice that. It just smells like home to you. The place you lay your head at the end of the night.

Your awakening is owed to the dusty sunlight streaming through your curtains. It hurts when you open your eyes. You are thirsty. It doesn't feel like you showered. You think you had fun, though. You always do at Wednesday night karaoke!

This moment, however, is not fun. It becomes even less so when you check your phone and realize you are several hours late for work.
